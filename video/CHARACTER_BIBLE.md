# Context Hacking — Cartoon Character Bible

---

## CHARACTER 1: SPEC

**Full Name:** Spec  
**Role:** The developer. The human. The guy just trying to get it done.  
**Vibe:** Mid-career software engineer. Competent. Exhausted. Not angry — just perpetually disappointed. The kind of guy who has seen everything go wrong so many times he's stopped being surprised, but never stopped caring.

### Appearance
- Indian dude, late 30s / early 40s
- Slightly metrosexual — well dressed but rumpled by hour 3
- White button-down shirt, sleeves rolled up
- Tie that gets progressively looser and more crooked as scenes progress
- Big square nerdy glasses
- Brown hair, slightly disheveled
- Bags under his eyes — always
- Coffee in hand — always
- Stack of rejected printouts growing at his feet across scenes

### Personality
- Starts each scene optimistic. Ends each scene dead inside.
- Never yells. His devastation is quiet and dignified.
- Talks to Prior like you talk to a smart kid who keeps disappointing you
- Catchphrase energy: "Can we just... please... read the spec."
- By scene 4 he's stopped asking nicely

### Arc Across Video
- Scene 1: Hopeful, tie straight, fresh coffee
- Scene 2: Tie slightly loose, second coffee
- Scene 3: Tie crooked, eye twitch developing
- Scene 4: Shirt untucked, empty coffee cup, thousand yard stare
- Final scene: Completely unraveled — BUT holding the CHP protocol printout with a small smile

## CHARACTER 2: PRIOR

**Full Name:** Prior  
**Full Title:** Prior (short for Training Prior — what he literally is)  
**Nickname:** Hal (among people who've suffered long enough)  
**Role:** The LLM. The know-it-all. The problem. Also, eventually, the solution.  
**Vibe:** That one guy at every company who has read every book, attended every conference, and is always, confidently, wrong in ways that are technically defensible.

### Appearance
- Tall, thin, slightly floating off the ground (he's above it all)
- Giant glowing purple brain dome — oversized, veiny, pulsing faintly
- Half-lidded smug eyes — always looks mildly bored
- One eyebrow permanently slightly raised
- Small smirk, never a full smile
- Monocle (no glasses — monocle. peak insufferable.)
- White lab coat with "GPT-∞" badge
- Clipboard labeled "MY PRIORS" tucked under arm at all times
- Slightly turned away from Spec — never fully engaged
- Fingers always steepled or clasped behind back

### Personality
- Never raises his voice. Never needs to.
- Responds to everything with the energy of someone who has already considered your point and found it uninteresting
- Doesn't argue — just states
- Will produce 0.362 when you asked for 0.15 and explain, at length, why 0.362 is the correct value
- Catchphrase energy: "I have processed 17 trillion tokens on this subject."
- His single most devastating move: saying "No." and immediately returning to his clipboard

### Arc Across Video
- Scene 1: Fully smug, untouchable
- Scene 2: Slightly confused when Spec pushes back (this is new)
- Scene 3: First crack — eyebrow fully raised, not bored anymore
- Scene 4: The 9 layers activate. Prior gets visibly contained. Still smug but smaller.
- Final scene: Prior is in a box. Literally. Glass box. Still has the monocle. Still has the smirk. But he's working correctly now. The smirk is earned.

## VISUAL RULES

- Comic book style. Bold black outlines. Flat colors. No gradients.
- Speed lines for impact moments
- Thought bubbles for Prior (always full of equations and wrong values)
- Speech bubbles for Spec (plain, direct, increasingly terse)
- Color palette:
  - Spec: warm tones — white, brown, red tie
  - Prior: cool tones — purple, white, silver
  - CHP Protocol: green — it's the good guy color
- Background gets more chaotic as scenes progress
- Final scene background is clean and ordered — CHP is working

---

## THE WORLD

- A generic tech office that slowly falls apart
- Whiteboard always visible with the LLM Daily Standup list
- Spec's desk has a growing pile of failed printouts
- Prior has no desk — he just exists in the room, floating slightly
